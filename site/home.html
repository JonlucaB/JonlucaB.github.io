About Me

Hello,

My name is Jonluca, and this is my personal website. This site is to act as a personal portfolio for my experiences, my music, and anything else I find interest in.
(Perhaps a Journal as well when you compare my typing and hand writing skills...)

I live with my two cats, Peanut and Butter, in San Antonio, Texas. Other than giving me a job in domestic disastor prevention, they are great roomates

I have been a computer scientist at the Southwest Research Institute since June 2023 after I graduated from Trinity University with a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science

Music is the second reason I made this website.
I have been playing percussion since 2012, and after 2019 my focus has largely been on the drumset and with my project Retro Cowgirl. Music composition has never been my strong suit, and social media feels like my enemy, so here you will find things that I have an opinion on, create, and enjoy playing.

Perhaps more will find its way here in the future, for as we all know, change is permanent.
